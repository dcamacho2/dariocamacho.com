Product Design Intern

Summer 2016

Overview

For the summer of 2016 I had the amazing opportunity to work for the Uber design team in San Francisco. In my stay I worked with truly creative and awesome people on all kinds of projects, and I had an absolute blast doing so. In the 16 weeks there, I worked on projects with three different teams: CRM, Paid Media, and .COM. Touching various parts of design from marketing to product design.

*Some work isn’t shown due to confidentiality and privacy agreements.

*Some designs are not currently live.

Uber.com

The majority of my internship I spent working with the Uber.com team. I was in charge of:

  • Creating and updating branded illustrations used on Uber.com
  • Design explorations for website components (eg: Fare Estimator) and optimize them for mobile, tablet, and desktop
  • Wireframing and creating high fidelity mockups of pages within uber.com/ride and uber.com/drive
  • Specking wireframes for easier handofs to developers and fixing wireframes that did not follow Uber guidelines

CRM

On the CRM team I worked with very talented communication designers on email designs and explorations. I worked on the design of "new" and "different" layouts for the announcement of new products for drivers and riders, the reason behind these explorations was to discover a more exiting look for the company's emails in order to increase their open rates and click through ratios. Here I was also in charge of designing and developing a new website for design guidelines to make it easier for Uber designers across the world to create emails that display Uber's superior design and be on brand.

Paid Media

Finally, with the Paid Media team, I helped develop new designs for digital creatives on Facebook, Instagram, and Google. I was in charge of creating a new wave of ads based on "Summer Fun", apart from designing I worked with copyrighters on team to find captivating images that closely matched the tone of the design.
