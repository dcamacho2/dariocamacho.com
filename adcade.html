Overview

For a year and half, I worked for Adcade in their New York office. Adcade is startup that focuses on designing and developing HTML5 ads as both a studio and also as a leader in their development of an Ad Builder for clients to use. In my time there I worked with the web team and the paid media. Having been my first job, Adcade helped me grow tremendously as a designer and developer.

Web Team

I began my experience at Adcade working on the Web Team. Initially I helped updating the CSS, HTML, and JavaScript of the website, however I was brought into the design team soon after. I helped go through two redesigns of the landing page and was in charge of the Desktop portion for both. Some of my tasks included:

  • Design explorations for website landing page and other experiences within the site (eg: login)
  • Wireframing and creating mockups of pages in low and high fidelity
  • Specking wireframes for easier handoffs to developers and fixing wireframes that did not follow guidelines
  • Used HTML, CSS, and, JavaScript to constantly update the look and functionality of the site. Based on my own design and those of others.
